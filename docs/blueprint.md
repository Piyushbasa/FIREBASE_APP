# **App Name**: AgriAssist

## Core Features:

- Weather Data: Display current weather conditions, including temperature, humidity, wind speed, and precipitation for the selected location.
- Sunrise/Sunset Times: Show sunrise and sunset times for effective planning of daily farming activities.
- Commodity Price Tracking: Display prices for various commodities such as rice, corn, grapes, potatoes, and olives. This tool uses reasoning to show the current price for the farmer's specific location.
- Field Monitoring: Allow farmers to upload and monitor their field images or connect with satellite data for real-time field status.
- AI Crop Suggestion: Suggest optimal crops to plant based on location, soil conditions, and weather patterns, presented through generative AI.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft olive green (#A9B449) to represent nature and agriculture.
- Background color: Very light beige (#F5F5DC) for a natural, earthy feel.
- Accent color: Muted gold (#BDB76B) to highlight key information and interactive elements.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif, combines a modern look with warmth; for both headlines and body text.
